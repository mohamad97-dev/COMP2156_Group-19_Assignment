George Brown College is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario.
It offers diploma, advanced diploma, and degree programs.
The college focuses on career-oriented and practical education.
Students gain hands-on experience through applied learning.

George Brown College supports diversity and inclusive education.

George Brown College was founded in 1967.
It offers programs in business, technology, health sciences, and community services.
The college focuses on hands-on training and career readiness.
